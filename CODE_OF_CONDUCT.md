# Code of Conduct

Loop Engineering contributors are expected to keep collaboration professional, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and kind.
- Focus feedback on the work.
- Give actionable review notes.
- Share context that helps others verify decisions.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and user data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities before maintainers can respond.
- Intentionally disruptive or misleading contributions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the project and its contributors.
